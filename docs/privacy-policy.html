Privacy Policy for VeloZones

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

Introduction

VeloZones ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our cycling training application available on iOS and iPadOS.

Key Point: All your training data stays on your device. We don't transmit your personal information to any remote servers.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Training Data: Your Functional Threshold Power (FTP), training plans, workouts, and workout completion status
  • Profile Information: Your name or nickname for personalization
  • Audio Preferences: Your audio coaching preference settings

Automatically Collected Information

  • Purchase Information: When you purchase VeloZones Pro, Apple processes your payment and provides us with a transaction receipt to verify your subscription status. We do not have access to your payment card details.

How We Store Your Information

All data you create in VeloZones is stored locally on your device using:

  • UserDefaults for user preferences and profile information
  • Local file storage for custom workouts and training plans

We do not transmit your personal data to any remote servers. Your training data, FTP settings, and workout information remain entirely on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide core app functionality (training plans, workout tracking, zone calculations)
  • Verify your VeloZones Pro subscription status
  • Restore your purchases across your devices
  • Personalize your training experience

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

The only external service we interact with is:

  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases and verifying subscription status. Apple's privacy policy governs how they handle payment and subscription information.

Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS/iPadOS system-level security features
  • Local device storage encryption (when enabled on your device)
  • No transmission of personal data over the internet

Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Delete Data: You can delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Export Workouts: VeloZones Pro users can export custom workouts as .vzw files
  • Modify Information: You can edit your profile, FTP, and training preferences at any time within the app

Children's Privacy

VeloZones is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy within the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of VeloZones after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

In-App Purchases

VeloZones Pro is offered as an in-app purchase subscription. All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We recommend reviewing Apple's Privacy Policy to understand how Apple handles payment information.

What VeloZones Pro Includes:

  • Unlimited custom workouts
  • Access to training plan library
  • Import/export functionality for workouts

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as you have VeloZones installed. When you delete the app, all local data is removed from your device according to iOS/iPadOS data management policies.

International Users

VeloZones is available worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, no data transfers across borders occur through our app.

Technical Details

For transparency, here are the technical details of data storage in VeloZones:

Local Storage

  • UserDefaults: User profile (FTP, name, audio preferences, onboarding status, current plan)
  • FileDocument: Custom workout export/import (.vzw files)

Apple Services

  • StoreKit: In-app purchase and subscription management
  • App Store: Transaction verification and restore purchases

No Analytics or Tracking

  • No third-party analytics frameworks
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No crash reporting services
  • No user behavior tracking

Permissions

VeloZones does not request access to:

  • Location services
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • HealthKit
  • Other sensitive device features

The only system permission required is for file import/export when using the Pro subscription features.
